The Catskills

The diverse region of the Catskills in southern New York is a short drive from the NYC/NJ/CT metro area, northeastern Pennsylvania, the Capital Region of NY, and western Massachusetts and Connecticut.

Historical Road Trip Through the Catskills

Pack your car and head for the Catskills! With historic sites and iconic cultural locations, discover the history of the region and enjoy breath-taking views during a road trip through the mountains and countryside of the Catskills.
